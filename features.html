FAQ

Fast answers — not essays.

Do I own my site and content?

Yes. Your pages and copy are yours. You can export and publish wherever your plan supports.

What does “Plan → Apply → Rollback” mean?

VoiceToWebsite previews the exact changes first. You approve before any write happens. Undo restores the last applied change.

Will this hurt performance with heavy scripts?

No. The system defaults to lazy-loading and reduced motion when requested. Lighthouse targets are a core product requirement.
